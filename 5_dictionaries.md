# Dictionaries

- Syntax: 
>`dict_name={'key':value,....}`

- To print:	
> `print(dict_name['key'])`
> `print(dict_name.get(key))`
> `print(dict_name.get(key,'message'))`

- To edit:		
>`dict_name.update({key:value})`

- To delete:	
> `del.dict_name[key]`
> `dict_name.pop(key)

- no.of keys:	
> `print(len(dict_name))`

- keys:		
> `print(dict_name.keys())`

- values:		
> `print(dict_name.values())`

- keys and values:	
> `print(dict_name.items())`
