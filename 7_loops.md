**Loops :**(aditya)

* <b>For</b> Loop :

```python3
nums=[1,2,3,4,5]

for i in nums :				# loops through the entire list
	body
	
for i in range(1,11,2):			# loops through 1-10 with a step of 2
	body
```

* <b>While</b> Loop :

```python3
while condition :
	body
```

* Jump statements : break, continue.
